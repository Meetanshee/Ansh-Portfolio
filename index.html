About Me

I am Ansh Sharma, a data analyst focused on converting raw business data into clear insights, practical dashboards, and decision-ready reports. I work with Excel, SQL, Python, and Power BI to clean messy datasets, identify trends, and present information in a way that business teams can understand quickly.
